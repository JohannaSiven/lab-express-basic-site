Bio

The Grumpy Cat (born Tardar Sauce) was one of Internet's most famous cat memes. Owing her fame originally to the Reddit community, who picked up on Grumpy's mom Tabitha's post in 2012 and turned her cute little grumpiness into a meme, she developed into an internet sensation with her own line of merchandise and millions of fans around the globe.

There was, however, much more to this fine lady than just her fabulous looks. Under the grumpy exterior one could find a cuddly, playful kitty who was always up for some belly rubs.

Grumpy cat continued to bring light and laughter to the public's lives until her sudden passing away in spring 2019.
